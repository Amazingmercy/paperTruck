How paperTruck Works

🌍Departments join the game by registering on the app.

🌍Upon registration, they can submit 20kg of paper to the school's game center.

🌍Each submitted 20kg of paper earns the department 10 points.

🌍Accumulated points contribute to the department's ranking.

🌍Departments have the opportunity to track their progress, earn rewards, and compare their points with others.
